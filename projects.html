NoSaints

New Dawn

A dark and atmospheric first-person shooter built on narrative and mystery. Set in a world of crumbling cities, towering airships, and fractured alliances, NoSaints tells its story through a unique perspective that puts you at the center of events you barely understand — until you do.

Built in Unity using a feature-oriented MVP architecture with Zenject dependency injection, KINEMATION procedural animation, and ScriptableObject-driven state machines.
